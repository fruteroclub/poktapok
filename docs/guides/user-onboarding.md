## Step-by-Step Onboarding

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet or Social Account

1. Visit [poktapok.com](https://poktapok.com) and click **"Connect Wallet"**
2. Choose your authentication method:
   - **Embedded Wallet**: Create a new wallet managed by Privy (easiest option)

3. Complete the authentication flow
4. You're now logged in with **incomplete** status

**URL**: `/onboarding` (automatically redirected after login)

  - Recommended: Square image, at least 400x400px

#### Location
- **City**: Your current city
- **Country**: Your current country

**Why we ask**: Helps us understand our global community and facilitate local connections.

## Common Questions

### Why was I approved as a guest instead of a member?

Guest status is the default approval path for first-time applicants. It ensures:
- Only committed users become full members
- Community remains high-quality and engaged
- You prove dedication through participation

**Think of it as**: Guest = "Welcome! Show us what you can do", Member = "You've proven your commitment"

### How long does it take to become a member?

**Typical timeline**: 4-8 weeks for active participants
- Depends on session frequency (usually 1-2 per week)
- Depends on activity completion rate
- Fastest path: Attend every session, complete activities quickly, maintain high quality

### Can I change my program after applying?

Not currently. You'll need to:
1. Complete your current program or withdraw
2. Apply for a new program
3. Program changes may be added in future updates

### What if I don't have a GitHub account?

GitHub is strongly recommended but not required. However:
- It significantly strengthens your application
- Shows technical background and coding activity
- Increases approval likelihood
- Consider creating one before applying

### Can I reapply if rejected?

Yes! You can reapply anytime with:
- Improved goal (more specific and measurable)
- Connected social accounts
- Completed GitHub profile
- Clearer alignment with program objectives

Review the rejection notes if provided by the admin.

### What happens if I'm inactive as a guest?

- Your account remains `guest` status
- You can resume participation anytime
- No automatic demotion or removal
- However, you won't progress toward membership

### Can I skip the guest tier?

Only in exceptional cases:
- Strong recommendation from trusted member
- Exceptional portfolio and proven track record
- Prior relationship with Poktapok team

Most users go through guest tier - it's not a negative, it's the standard path.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't submit my application

**Check**:
- All required fields are filled out (red asterisks)
- Goal is between 1-280 characters
- Username meets format requirements (3-20 chars, lowercase)
- You've selected a program
- You're connected to the internet

**Still stuck?**: Clear browser cache and try again

### My avatar upload failed

**Possible causes**:
- File size > 5MB (compress your image)
- Unsupported format (use JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- Network issue (check your connection)

**Solution**: Use an image compression tool like [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/) before uploading

### I want to change my username after submission

**Unfortunately**: Usernames cannot be changed once set. This is by design for identity consistency.

**Alternative**: If you haven't been approved yet, you can withdraw your application and reapply with a new profile.

### How do I check my application status?

1. Log in to your account
2. Your status is displayed in the navbar (top right)
3. Possible statuses: `incomplete`, `pending`, `guest`, `active`, `rejected`

### Who do I contact for help?

- **Technical issues**: Check [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/your-repo/poktapok/issues)
- **Application questions**: Email support@poktapok.com
- **Community support**: Join our Discord/Telegram (links in footer)
